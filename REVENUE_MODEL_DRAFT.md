# House Of Wax Revenue Model Draft

## Starting Revenue Model

Recommended starting model:

- 9% marketplace commission
- 10% auction commission
- optional promoted listings
- no monthly seller fee at first

## Future Revenue Streams

- seller subscriptions
- featured seller placement
- homepage promotion
- auction event fees
- seller analytics tools
- music/culture sponsorships
- email/social promotion packages

## Example

If an item sells for $100 with a 9% commission, House Of Wax earns $9 before payment processing and operating costs.

If an auction sells for $250 with a 10% commission, House Of Wax earns $25 before payment processing and operating costs.
