# House Of Wax V10 Sales Upgrade

This version focuses on making the public storefront more professional and easier to shop.

## New customer-facing upgrades

- Product card grid instead of long text listings
- Thumbnails shown directly on listing cards
- Search, genre filter, condition filter, and sorting
- Product detail view with larger media area
- Request Hold button on each product detail
- Internet media previews remain visible instead of hidden behind links only

## Branding upgrades

- Store name
- Tagline
- Announcement bar
- Logo upload or logo URL
- Banner/hero image upload or URL
- Instagram URL
- Contact email and phone

## Admin workflow

Go to Admin Login → Dashboard → Storefront Branding & Sales Settings.

Upload your logo/banner or paste direct image URLs, then save.

## Efficiency improvements

Internet Media Finder now tries to auto-detect the source and media type from pasted URLs. YouTube thumbnails and direct image thumbnails are auto-filled where possible.
