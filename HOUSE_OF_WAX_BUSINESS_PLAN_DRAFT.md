# House Of Wax Business Plan Draft

## Executive Summary

House Of Wax is a seller-powered music and culture marketplace where approved sellers create their own stores, list music/culture products, set prices, handle shipping, and serve buyers. House Of Wax provides the platform, marketplace rules, buyer/seller accountability, flagged listing review, auction eligibility, discovery tools, culture content, and revenue systems.

## Business Model

House Of Wax does not need to own inventory. Sellers own and manage their products.

House Of Wax generates revenue through:

- marketplace commission
- auction commission
- seller subscriptions
- promoted listings
- featured seller placement
- auction event fees
- culture sponsorships

## Buyer/Seller Trust Model

Both buyers and sellers are accountable. Sellers are approved before selling. Buyers register before buying. Both sides can be rated, restricted, or suspended.

## Auction Model

Auctions are earned, not automatically available. Sellers must reach performance thresholds or receive admin override.

## Competitive Inspiration

House Of Wax combines:
- Discogs-style music marketplace/data energy
- eBay-style seller stores and buyer/seller accountability
- Carolina Soul-style auction trust and curation
- Popsike-style pricing/research inspiration

## Key Differentiation

House Of Wax is not just a record inventory app. It is a music and culture marketplace where independent sellers can build their own storefronts within a trusted platform.
