# Buyer Policy Draft

Buyers must register to buy, bid, message, leave feedback, or open disputes. Buyers are expected to pay for purchases and auctions they commit to, read listings and seller policies, communicate respectfully, and avoid abusing returns, disputes, chargebacks, or feedback. Buyers may receive ratings, unpaid order strikes, restrictions, or suspensions. Buyer remorse does not automatically override seller policies when an item was accurately described and properly shipped.
