# House Of Wax Buyer Policy Draft

## Buyer Registration

Buyers must register before buying, bidding, messaging, saving items, reporting listings, or opening disputes.

## Buyer Responsibilities

Buyers must:

- read listings carefully
- review seller policies
- pay for purchase commitments
- pay for auction wins
- communicate respectfully
- provide accurate contact/shipping details
- avoid abusive returns, false claims, and chargebacks

## Buyer Ratings

House Of Wax may track:

- rating
- completed purchases
- unpaid orders
- disputes
- strikes
- account status

## Buyer Statuses

- New Buyer
- Verified Buyer
- Trusted Buyer
- Restricted Buyer
- Suspended Buyer

## Non-Paying Buyers

A buyer who requests a purchase, wins an auction, or accepts a deal and does not pay may receive an unpaid order mark and strike.

Repeated nonpayment can limit buying, bidding, messaging, or platform access.
