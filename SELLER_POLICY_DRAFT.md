# House Of Wax Seller Policy Draft

## Seller Approval

Sellers must apply and be approved before selling.

## Seller Freedom

Approved sellers manage:

- store profile
- store policies
- products
- prices
- shipping
- customer service
- order updates
- social posts

## Seller Policies

Sellers may create their own policies, including:

- shipping
- returns
- grading
- customer service
- buyer requirements
- bundle/discounts
- local pickup
- international shipping
- auctions

Seller policies may be stricter or more generous than House Of Wax rules, but never weaker.

## Listing Review

Fixed-price listings do not require manual approval. Listings can be reported and reviewed by House Of Wax.

## Seller Accountability

Sellers may be warned, restricted, suspended, or removed for counterfeits, scams, repeated misgrading, failure to ship, abusive communication, or fee avoidance.
